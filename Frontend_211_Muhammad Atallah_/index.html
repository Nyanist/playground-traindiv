Orion

Orion is one of the most recognizable constellations, named for the mythological hunter in Greek myth. It is prominent in the winter sky and is visible from most of the world because it lies near the celestial equator. Key features include the three bright stars of Orion's Belt and prominent stars like the red supergiant Betelgeuse and the blue supergiant Rigel. Scorpio

Scorpio is a zodiac constellation located in the southern sky. Its distinctive “curved tail” shape makes it one of the easiest to recognize.
