Orion

Orion is one of the most recognizable constellations, named for the mythological hunter in Greek myth.

Key features include the three bright stars of Orion's Belt and prominent stars like Betelgeuse and Rigel.

Scorpio

Scorpio is a zodiac constellation in the southern celestial hemisphere.

It is most visible during summer in the Northern Hemisphere, known for its red supergiant star Antares.
